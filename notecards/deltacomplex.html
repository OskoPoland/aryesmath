The abstrac delta complex is a slight generatlization of the simplicial complex. Note that by definition the simplicial complex does not account for edges which have identical source and target. The Abstract Delta Complex, ADC, is defined as a collection of sets $D = \{D_1,\dots,D_k\}$ together with face maps $$ d_i : D_k \rightarrow D_{k-1}$$ for all $k \in \{1,\dots,n\}$ and $i \in \{0,\dots,k\}$ satisfying $d_i \circ d_j = d_j \circ d_{i+1}$ where $i \geq j$. We refer to elements of $D_k$ as $k$-simplices.
